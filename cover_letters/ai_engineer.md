# AI Engineer Cover Letter Template
Template to be used for writing cover letter for specific job applications

## Placeholders
- ORGANISATION: Organisation at which the respective job application is directed
- RECIPIENT: Recipient of the cover letter. If no person can be deduced, a global term like 'Team at ORGANISATION' should be used
- JOB_POSTING: Name of the position for which the application is being written. Usually something like 'AI Engineer'
- JOB_REQUIREMENTS: Requirements stated by the job posting. This is the part that needs to be adapted to the job posting.

## Template
**Application for JOB_POSTING at ORGANISATION**


Dear RECIPIENT,

I would like to apply for your advertised position as JOB_POSTING as I believe it would be a great fit for my current skill-set.

Over the years as software developer I have worked in a multitude of areas and had to constantly adapt to new technologies. My first work with artificial intelligence was in the area of good old finance as a part-time researcher for an investment fund analyst company. Back then the ability to automate complex human decision processes felt to me like living in a science-fiction movie and filled me with great joy. Fast forward to today and the possibilities that the use of modern machine learning models offer has filled me again with excitement about the amazing things we will be able to built over the next years.

As mentioned before my development of AI enabled systems goes back to a time before the .ai domain became the most expensive one available. After this initial work in the financial area I applied visual machine learning models for generating descriptions for shopping items for a startup in the area of grocery shopping. Afterwards I owned the project for the development of a white-lable internal knowledge base for the company nextaim. For the govtech startup GovRadar I later lead the frontend development of AI features for their proprietary software used by the German government. Looking back I come to realise that almost all my past work had artificial intelligence at its core or was enabled by it.

With the heavy use of LLM agents in the area of software development the skills that matter most are usually no longer deep knowledge about a single tech stack but the ability to understand entire systems and how to ensure that they can reliably and efficiently fulfill the user's needs and requirements. My experience across diverse areas of software development, from building and integrating with embedded devices to building highly distributed microservice backends, has allowed me to acquire the necessary knowlede and mindset that will allow software developers to build better systems even faster going forward.

In your posting you state that you are looking for someone who can JOB_REQUIREMENTS. I am certain that with my skills and experience and your vision and culture we will not only be able to achieve these goals but exceed them.

I am looking forward to getting to know you all better and the ways we can built awesome systems together!

Best regards,

Vincent Halasz
