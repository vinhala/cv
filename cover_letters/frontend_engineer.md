# Frontend Engineer Cover Letter Template
Template to be used for writing cover letter for specific job applications

## Placeholders
- ORGANISATION: Organisation at which the respective job application is directed
- RECIPIENT: Recipient of the cover letter. If no person can be deduced, a global term like 'Team at ORGANISATION' should be used
- JOB_POSTING: Name of the position for which the application is being written. Usually something like 'AI Engineer'
- JOB_FIT: Arguments why I am a perfect fit for the job posting in one or two sentences, founded in experience and skills.

## Template
**Application for JOB_POSTING at ORGANISATION**


Dear RECIPIENT,

I would like to apply for your advertised position as JOB_POSTING as I believe it would be a great fit for my current skill-set.

Over the years as software developer I have worked in a multitude of areas that allowed me to work on the design and development user interface for a diverse set of users. Even though each group of users has different requirements the process of determining their needs and the best way to fulfill them is reproducable and is what has given me great joy over the past. Developing user interfaces is a great responsibility as it determines the usefulness of a system perceived by its users. Even the best engineering on the internals of a system will be futile if the user interface does not fit the users' behavior. 

I am sure you will find me to be a great fit for the open position because JOB_FIT

I am looking forward to getting to know you all better and the ways we can built awesome systems together!

Best regards,

Vincent Halasz
