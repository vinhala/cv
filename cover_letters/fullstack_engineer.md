# Fullstack Engineer Cover Letter Template
Template to be used for writing cover letter for specific job applications

## Placeholders
- ORGANISATION: Organisation at which the respective job application is directed
- RECIPIENT: Recipient of the cover letter. If no person can be deduced, a global term like 'Team at ORGANISATION' should be used
- JOB_POSTING: Name of the position for which the application is being written. Usually something like 'AI Engineer'
- JOB_FIT: Arguments why I am a perfect fit for the job posting in one or two sentences, founded in experience and skills.

## Template (in draft)
**Application for JOB_POSTING at ORGANISATION**


Dear RECIPIENT,

I would like to apply for your advertised position as JOB_POSTING as I believe it would be a great fit for my current skill-set.

Over the years as software developer I have worked in a multitude of areas and had to constantly adapt to new technologies. Even though the technologies changed, the underlying process of software engineering did not. I believe developing software is not about understanding the frameworks it uses, the users' problems it tries to solve or the infrastructure it runs on. Modern software development is about all of these and how to combine them to improve people's lives.

In the modern era of software development the skills that matter most are the ability to understand entire systems and how to ensure that they can reliably and efficiently fulfill the user's needs and requirements. My experience across diverse areas of software development, from building and integrating with embedded devices to building highly distributed microservice backends, has allowed me to acquire the necessary knowlede and mindset that is required for being a true fullstack developer.

I am sure you will find me to be a great fit for the open position because JOB_FIT

Looking forward to getting to know you all better and the ways we can built awesome systems together!

Best regards,

Vincent Halasz
